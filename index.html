powerdelivery


A free, powerful toolkit enabling teams continuously deliver software to their customers using Microsoft Team Foundation Server and Windows PowerShell.

Reduce cycle time

How do you know that users love your idea? Get it out to there and listen. Automate your deployment once and move it through your development, test, and production quality gates fast to get that feedback.

Release with confidence

Making the release process a policy isn't enough. Automate it and use technology to stop the team from making changes to servers directly.

Bring peace to the team

Stop wasting time chasing down differences in server configuration and focus your resources on what they do best - innovate and create value for your customers.

Get more insight from your delivery process.

Quickly build a custom delivery process for your software using Windows PowerShell that surfaces key deployment and quality information for your IT operations and development team.

Stop untested changes from making it into production

Don't take chances with deploying changes. Powerdelivery forces the team to deploy the same release candidates from Development, to Test and then on to Production to prevent environments from getting out of synch.

A powerful, but simple syntax

Powerdelivery is designed to enable even beginners using Windows PowerShell to quickly put together a completely custom, one-click automated deployment that follows best practices of Continuous Delivery.

Use and create delivery modules to save scripting time

Included are several powerful modules for automating time consuming, manual deployment tasks through configuration alone. Powerdelivery leverages the simple YAML language for configuration settings.
